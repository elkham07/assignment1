Tribute to Steve Jobs

1955 – 2011

About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs (1955–2011) was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and co-founder of Apple Inc. He is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution and for leading the creation of iconic devices such as the iPhone, iPad, and Macintosh. His vision reshaped technology, design, and the way we interact with digital products.

Major Events

  1. 1955 – Born in San Francisco, California.
  2. 1976 – Co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak.
  3. 1984 – Introduced the Macintosh computer.
  4. 1997 – Returned to Apple as CEO.
  5. 2007 – Launched the iPhone.
  6. 2011 – Passed away, leaving a legacy of innovation.

Key Contributions

  • Co-founder of Apple Inc.
  • Introduced the iPhone, iPad, and iPod.
  • Helped revolutionize personal computing.
  • Founder of Pixar Animation Studios.
